Program In A Nutshell

TrueMD is dedicated to helping you reach your potential for improved health, increase your lifespan, and improve your quality of life as you age. Our goal is to help you accomplish this through three main concepts offered through TrueMD Affiliates True Hormone Pellet Therapy, The True Weight-loss program, and True Wellness Concepts. Both men and women can benefit from our programs and can enjoy a boost in energy, improved sexual satisfaction, less age-related symptoms and potentially protecting you from those debilitating diseases that you could get as you age.

Affiliates of TrueMD are committed to helping you live happier, longer, and with more enjoyment of life. All are certified to provide this through extensive training programs at the TrueMD headquarters in Colleyville, TX Each is highly trained and is passionate in helping you get the services you need. Everyone wants to get older and “age”.None of us want to die prematurely, but you want to age with quality of life: enjoy better sex, vitality, energy, activity, clarity of mind, eating well, and social connections.

Chief Medical Officer

Robert L. True, MD, is the founder and CEO of TrueMD He is and has been passionate about providing preventive care, i e. His training has given Dr. True a comprehensive background in age management through multiple complimentary specialties He graduated from Pharmacy school at Idaho State University in Pocatello, Idaho, where he was born and raised From this training, he learned about medications, herbal supplements, and compounding pharmaceutical products that helped him understand and be able to formulate the best combination of these for you..
